My journey at Andela Rwanda

I got interested in the andela program because of all the success stories that I kept on hearing from my friends. It all started from a rumor that one of our highschool classmates had recently purchased a car after just one year from finishing the program. Knowing the guy I called him up and asked him personally if the rumors were true to which he vigorously denied. However, besides him not buying a car his actual story was not far from impressive on its own. He suggested that I should join the next cohort and after a long conversation he gave me the application link and the rest is history. After about two months or so since I joined the program I can safely say that this has been a true Oasis of growth. On a technical point of view, I got to enhance my skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript to a whole new level and even got to learn about figma and node js which in turn helped me build my first fully functional website. Pretty awesome right! And on a personal note, I got to meet people with the same passion and drive that I have and these people later on became my friends, some even closer than I expected. So to me, never mind the car, as long as I get to see myself grow and make a few good friends along the way I am good.
